Volunteer as Worker

STEP 1 Become a Corona-Warrior

You can volunteer as a worker at one of the Janta Quarantine Facilities and help India in the fight against COVID-19.

Get started by completing the following digital course on COVID-19 to help you deal with the crisis and earn your free skill certificate.

Course Outline
STEP 2 Submit your Registration

Complete the registration form below and submit all the required information.

Registration Form
STEP 3 Verification and Approval

Our team will look through your information and accordingly approve your application.

You may also be required to go through an online interview.

STEP 4 Deployment

You will be assigned a Quarantine Facility near you to volunteer at.

Provide your Site

STEP 1 Offer your Site to be made a Quarantine Facility

You can voluntary offer a site that can function as a quarantine facility for isolating people.

Registration Form
STEP 2 Approval and On-Site Inspection

Once your application is approved, our team will conduct an inspection of the site.

The site will also be sanitized and provided with all necessary medical supplies before it is added to our directory.

STEP 3 Workers

If your facility requires more workers, we will direct our volunteers onto your site.

STEP 4 Facility Portal

You will be provided access to the Facility Portal for managing patients and communicating with our main portal.

Donate for our Heroes

Every bit of money you donate will be spent to incentivize Janta Quarantine workers and facility owners.
While nothing can equalize with their service, your contribution will go a long way in encouraging them to continue serving us all.



Alternatively, you can also donate to PM Cares Fund.
